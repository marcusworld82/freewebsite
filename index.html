About Us

Serving Cleveland
Since 1991

Franciscus Roofing has been protecting homes and businesses across Greater Cleveland for over 34 years. What started as a commitment to honest, high-quality workmanship has grown into one of Northeast Ohio's most trusted roofing companies.

We are a veteran-owned business, and that means something to us. We bring the same discipline, attention to detail, and sense of duty to every single project we take on, whether it's a quick repair or a full replacement.

With more than 18,000 satisfied customers, a 4.8 Google rating, and BBB A+ accreditation, our record speaks for itself. We are available 24 hours a day, respond in minutes, and never charge for estimates.
